## 👓 Featured Group Project: MindSense

[cite_start]In addition to my individual development work, I collaborated on **MindSense** [cite: 94][cite_start]—an innovative health-tech solution designed to bridge the gap in early mental health detection[cite: 42].

* [cite_start]🚀 **Project Overview:** MindSense is a pair of AI-powered smart spectacles featuring discreet, built-in EEG brainwave sensors [cite: 50] [cite_start]that monitor brain activity to detect emotional distress in real time[cite: 47, 50].
* [cite_start]🧠 **AI Multi-Model Pipeline:** Processes raw EEG signals sequentially through a **CNN-based autoencoder** (noise filtering) [cite: 60][cite_start], an **SVM model** (feature extraction) [cite: 61][cite_start], and a **TCN model** (time-series emotional state prediction) [cite: 62] [cite_start]deployed via TensorFlow Serving[cite: 64].
* [cite_start]🌱 **ESG-Driven Architecture:** Engineered to deliver profound **Social Value** by providing accessible, stigma-free tracking[cite: 48, 79]; [cite_start]**Environmental Benefits** via low-energy wearable design [cite: 81][cite_start]; and **Strict Governance** prioritizing transparent algorithms and secure data encryption[cite: 83].
